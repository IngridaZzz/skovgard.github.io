Most people think those massive high rise buildings you see in China are dull and ugly but for some reason they have a lot of character. Especially the filthy ones. This piece was made to convey that feeling. To create this piece, artist was inspired by Hong Kong, Chongqing and Shanghai. It really gives you vibes of busy city, the sun and the foggy feeling… am I in China?
