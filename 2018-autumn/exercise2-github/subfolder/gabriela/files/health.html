Health insurance


Let's see how to keep health insurence






Each state has own system of social security and health insurance, and is regulated by its legislation.


Coordination of providing health insurance in Europe union is regulated by EU regulations.


According to EC No. 883/2004: Nr. 11: 1) you can not have health insurance in more than one EU member countries.


Of course there are always some exeptions. Some insurance companies can give you a "student" status.That mean that while you are the student you can stay insured there. So everytime ask if they not offer this possibility.


Danish national health legislation:


• going to study /studying for more than 1 year - you have health insurance payed by DK

• working over 9 hours / week - you have health insurance payed by Denmark

• going to study for 1 year and less - you have only right to get health treatment in Denmark, which would be refunded by home insurance company through your Blue Card


Information ''going to study for more than 1 year / 1 year and less'', is given by you, in formula Providing information about home insurance country, that you are asked to fill in shortly after arriving to DK, by Udbetaling DK.


If you are not sure about you have / have not Health Insurance payed by Denmark, contact Udbetaling DK.
