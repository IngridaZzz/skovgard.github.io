llustrator and Digital Photographer Alberto Seveso was born in Milan, he grow up in Sardinia but is now working and living in Bristol (UK) as a freelancer. His passion for graphic art started when he was in a young age and he was really fascinated by the graphic of skate decks and the cover of music CD of metal bands in the early ‘90s. From this passion he started to create his artworks.

This illustration epresent love and passion, while he adds his own steam punk type of feeling to it. They look like robots but at the same time you get the feeling that they are alive. He uses soft shapes and colors to make it look more nature like. It looks like cloud plant like mixture pattern that makes you wonder are they human or…?

The beauty of a woman represented trough cloud like design makes you feel like it genie from a bottle. Mixture of beauty, mystery and sensuality calms and make you feel at peace. It’s not only beautiful piece of design, it is creative and different. Fog texture makes you wonder, how is it made?

Light and darkness, beauty and fear, human and inhuman. The contrasts of these photos are very visible. The way colors are used although shapes are similar, creates light and darkness, alive and dead feeling. While the colors create feeling of butterflies and liveliness, gray, black colors and darkened eye sockets remove all the life from a human, making him look dead or inhuman. The power of color…
