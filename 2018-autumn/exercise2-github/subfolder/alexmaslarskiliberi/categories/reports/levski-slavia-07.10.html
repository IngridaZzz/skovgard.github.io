Levski Sofia vs. Slavia Sofia 07.10.2018

The oldest Sofia Derby!

ADMIN / REPORTS / 08.10.2018 / Images: SectorBvideo, Block 5, Sportal.bg

Over 10 000 People and pyro from Levski fans!

The oldest Sofia derby was played in the Georgi Asparuhov Stadium. Levski Sofia is in the first place and this game was very important after the derby win last week. The supporters almost filled the stands with over 10 000 people attending the game. Once again Ultras Levski are divided in two parts. Sofia West and the majority stay in the traditional stand Sector B while Sout Division were placed in Block 5 in Sector A. They were supported also by other friendly groups such as Levski&Techno, Ruse and others who don't approve the state of Sector B at the moment.
